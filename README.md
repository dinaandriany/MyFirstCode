# TalkyToast Lab 🍞🎤✨

Welcome to the **TalkyToast Lab**! This is a Kawaii-themed, interactive 60-second speaking challenge designed to make practicing English fluency fun, engaging, and highly effective.

## 🌟 Inspiration
This project was built as an entry for the **#MyFirstWebsite** challenge on Goodwall using GitHub Copilot. It was created out of a personal need: to keep English speaking skills sharp and confident in a fun way, especially for daily use and preparing for an upcoming role as an English-speaking nanny at Rumah Anak Surga.

## 🕹️ Features
Instead of just reading static text, this mini-app functions as a complete speaking practice workflow:
* **Gachapon Topic Picker:** Generates random speaking prompts, including daily life and childcare scenarios.
* **15-Second Think Time:** A dedicated preparation timer to gather your thoughts.
* **60-Second Speaking Timer:** A visual countdown with a pulsing background effect for the final 10 seconds.
* **One-Click AI Feedback:** Instantly copies a pre-formatted prompt to your clipboard so you can easily ask AI tools (like Gemini or ChatGPT) to review your grammar and suggest vocabulary improvements.
* **Kawaii UI:** A soft, cozy design using pure HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript.

## 🚀 How to Play
1. Click **"Draw a Topic Capsule! 🔴"** to get your prompt.
2. Use the **15-second "Think Time"** to prepare your answer.
3. Grab your phone's voice recorder. When the screen says **"🔴 RECORDING!"**, start speaking for 60 seconds!
4. When the timer ends, click **"📋 Copy AI Feedback Prompt"** and paste it into your favorite AI along with your audio transcript to get immediate corrections.
